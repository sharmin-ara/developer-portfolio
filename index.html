I was born in December, 1974, in Rajshahi--a beautiful town of Bangladesh, situated at the bank of river Padma. Along with my three siblings I spent my first eighteen years in Rajshahi University campus as my parents were faculties of RU. Among the four I am the youngest one. After completion of H. Sc., I came to Dhaka to study EEE in BUET. I did my M. Sc. from Southern Illinoise University , Carbondale, USA and Ph. D from BUET. I used teach undergrad for the last twelve years. I love to travel with my family and friends, and watching movies.
